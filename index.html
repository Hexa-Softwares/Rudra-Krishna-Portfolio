Rudra Krishna

Electrical & Electronics Engineer | Power Systems Specialist

Rudra Krishna is an MSc Electrical and Electronics Engineering graduate from the University of Leicester, specializing in Power Systems, Power Electronics, and Digital Signal Processing. He has a solid foundation in control engineering, proficient in P, PI, and PID controller tuning, load flow analysis, and transformer and cable sizing. His hands-on experience at Delta ElMech in Hyderabad involved interpreting substation SLDs, designing HT/LT distribution networks, and ensuring adherence to safety and regulatory norms. Skilled in MATLAB/Simulink, ETAP, AutoCAD Electrical, and Revit, he demonstrates strong technical acumen in system modeling and simulation. Rudra has also contributed to research with multiple IEEE-indexed publications focusing on signal processing and machine learning applications. His certifications in MATLAB Onramp, Control Design, and Power Electronics Simulation further strengthen his analytical and computational proficiency. With a strong commitment to innovation and sustainable engineering, Rudra aims to leverage his expertise to deliver efficient and reliable solutions within the power and energy sectors.
